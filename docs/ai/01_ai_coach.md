# 01 AI Coach

## Purpose
The AI Coach is the voice of Orbit. It provides insights, encouragement, and tactical scheduling advice to help the user "Become Better Than Yesterday."

## Capabilities

### 1. Performance Analysis
- Analyzes correlations (e.g., "You score 20% higher on days you focus before 10 AM").
- Generates "Weekly Reviews" summarizing score trends.

### 2. Tactical Scheduling
- Suggests moving tasks based on the user's historical "Peak Focus" hours.
- Identifies "Risk Windows" (times when habits are usually missed).

### 3. Motivation
- Provides context-aware encouragement (e.g., "You're 15 points away from breaking your Weekly PR").

## Constraints
- **Privacy First**: All data passed to the AI must be anonymized or processed locally where possible.
- **Agency**: The AI cannot move tasks or delete habits without user confirmation.
- **Stoicism**: The AI should avoid toxic positivity and focus on actionable discipline.
