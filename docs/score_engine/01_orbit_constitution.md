# 01 Orbit Constitution

## Purpose
The Orbit Constitution defines the core values and immutable laws that govern the Orbit Personal Operating System. It ensures that every feature developed and every algorithm implemented aligns with the mission of self-actualization.

## Mission Statement
**"Become Better Than Yesterday."**

Orbit is built on the belief that the only meaningful progress is measured against one's previous self. By quantifying holistic productivity, health, and consistency, Orbit provides a mirror for the user's potential.

## Scope
This document applies to all software logic, AI behaviors, and user experience decisions within the Orbit platform.

## Definitions
- **The Self**: The only competitor recognized by the system.
- **Pillars**: The categories of human performance tracked (Tasks, Health, Focus, Habits, Planner, Goals).
- **Orbit Score**: A numerical representation of a day's holistic output.
- **Agency**: The user's ability to act and change their trajectory.

## Core Rules
1. **Zero Social Comparison**: There shall be no global leaderboards, social sharing of comparative rankings, or competitive features involving other users.
2. **Infinite Upward Mobility**: The Orbit Score is unlimited. There is no "perfect score," only "personal records."
3. **Holistic Integrity**: Growth in one area (e.g., Tasks) cannot fully compensate for total neglect in another (e.g., Sleep).
4. **Historical Sovereignty**: The user's past data is a sacred record. Recalculations due to engine updates must be transparent and user-approved.
5. **Human-Centric AI**: The AI Coach suggests and insights but never imposes or automatically reduces the user's workload without permission.

## Edge Cases
- **Data Erasure**: If a user deletes their history, the system must treat the next day as "Day 1" of a new journey, preserving the "Better Than Yesterday" logic starting from zero.
- **Extended Absence**: Life happens. The "5-day Grace Period" for streaks acknowledges that temporary breaks are part of a long-term journey.

## Future Considerations
As Orbit evolves into hardware or biological integrations, the Constitution will remain the filtering lens for how that data is used to reward the user.
